About Us

12+ Years Experience

Nelsons number 1 cleaning service provider.

At LSB Cleaning we provide a superior cleaning service with customer satisfaction being our number one priority. Owned by two sisters, one with 12 years’ experience in the cleaning industry, Michaela Neale-Blandford and the other, who is passionate about business and growing a willing and trustworthy business, Stephanie Marie.

We both created this company based on the immense experience and dedication. As Nelson is growing bigger each year, we aim to fulfil the needs for trustworthy, well-trained cleaners for your home, your business, or your holidays home! We want to bring a new type of cleaning to Nelson, a company hat appreciates pleasing their customers by doing the thing that you might not want to do, cleaning.
